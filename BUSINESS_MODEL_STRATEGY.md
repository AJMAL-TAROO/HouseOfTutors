## 1. Market Analysis

### 1.1 Mauritian Education Context

**Key Characteristics:**
- Population: ~1.3 million with strong emphasis on education
- Private tutoring is culturally accepted and widely used
- Parents invest significantly in children's education
- English and French are widely spoken (bilingual market)
- Growing internet penetration (60%+ of population)
- Competitive exam culture (CPE, SC, HSC) drives tutoring demand

**Target Segments:**
1. **Primary Level (Grades 1-6)**: CPE preparation, foundational learning
2. **Secondary Level (Forms 1-5)**: SC/HSC preparation, specialized subjects
3. **Tertiary Level**: University students, professional courses
4. **Others**: Adult learners, professional development

### 1.2 Competitive Landscape

| Competitor Type | Pricing Model | Your Advantage |
|-----------------|---------------|----------------|
| Traditional Tutors | Direct payment | Technology, convenience, scale |
| Tutoring Centers | Monthly fees | Lower overhead, flexible scheduling |
| Informal Networks | Variable | Quality assurance, structured content |

## 3. Recommended Model: Hybrid Commission + Subscription

### 3.1 The Optimal Strategy

After careful analysis, the **recommended approach** is a **Hybrid Model** that combines:

1. **Commission on Transactions (Primary Revenue)**: 15-20% platform fee
2. **Tutor Subscription Tiers (Secondary Revenue)**: For premium features
3. **Freemium Student Access**: Students pay for tutoring, not platform access

### 3.2 Why This Works for Mauritius

| Factor | Why It Works |
|--------|--------------|
| **Cultural Fit** | Mauritians value transparency in pricing; commission models are understood |
| **Trust Building** | Platform handling payments builds trust for parents |
| **Tutor Motivation** | Tutors earn based on performance, not fixed fees |
| **Your Marketing ROI** | You can track and optimize cost per acquisition |
| **Scalability** | Revenue grows with transaction volume |

As platform owner, you're investing capital in:
1. **Student Acquisition**: Advertising to find students
2. **Tutor Recruitment**: Attracting quality tutors
3. **Brand Building**: Creating trust in the platform

This investment is justified because:
- You provide the marketplace infrastructure
- You handle technology, support, and quality assurance
- You take the risk of building the platform
