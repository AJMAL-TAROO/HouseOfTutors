## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 What is House of Tutors?

House of Tutors is a comprehensive online tutoring platform that enables educators to:
- Create and manage virtual classrooms
- Track student attendance and progress
- Share educational materials and notes
- Conduct live online classes
- Manage timetables and schedules
- Provide feedback to students
- Review homework submissions

### 1.2 System Requirements

**Minimum Requirements:**
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge - latest versions)
- Stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps recommended)
- Device: Desktop, laptop, or tablet
- Screen resolution: 1024x768 or higher

**Recommended for Video Classes:**
- Webcam and microphone
- High-speed internet (10 Mbps or higher)
- Desktop or laptop computer
- Speakers or headphones

### 1.3 Before You Begin

Ensure you have received:
- Your admin email address
- Your password
- VR classroom link (for conducting online classes)

## 2. Account Login and Access

### 2.1 Accessing the Platform

**Step 1:** Open your web browser and navigate to:
```
https://www.housesoftutors.com
```

**Step 2:** Click on the "Admin Login" or "Tutor Login" button on the homepage.


### 2.2 Logging In

**Step 1:** Enter your registered email address in the "Email" field.

**Step 2:** Enter your password in the "Password" field.

**Step 3:** Click the "Login" button.

**Step 4:** Upon successful login, you will be redirected to your Admin Dashboard.

If this is your first time logging in:
1. You may be prompted to change your password
2. Review your profile information
3. Familiarize yourself with the dashboard layout

**Forgot Password:**
- Contact your administrator for password reset
- Check your email for password reset instructions

**Cannot Access Account:**
- Verify you're using the correct email address
- Ensure Caps Lock is off when entering password
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

## 5. Student Management

### 5.1 Creating a New Student Account

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Access Creation Page:**
   - Open the side menu
   - Click "Create Student"

2. **Fill Student Information:**
   - **Full Name:** Enter student's complete name
   - **Telephone:** Student's phone number
   - **Responsible Party:** Parent or guardian name
   - **Responsible Party Telephone:** Contact number
   - **Age:** Student's age
   - **Email:** Student's email address (must be unique)

3. **Generate Password:**
   - Click "Generate Password" button
   - A secure password will be automatically created
   - **IMPORTANT:** Copy this password and share it securely with the student

4. **Upload Student Data:**
   - Review all information for accuracy
   - Click "Upload" button
   - Wait for confirmation message
   - Record the student's credentials safely

**Important Notes:**
- Email addresses must be unique per student
- Passwords are randomly generated for security
- Share login credentials securely with students/parents
- Keep a secure record of student credentials

### 5.2 Adding Students to Classrooms

**Process:**

1. **Navigate to Assignment Page:**
   - Open side menu
   - Select "Add Student to Classroom"

2. **Select Student:**
   - Choose student from the dropdown list
   - Students are listed by name and email

3. **Select Classroom:**
   - Choose the classroom from the dropdown
   - Multiple classroom selections may be available

4. **Confirm Assignment:**
   - Click "Add Student to Classroom" button
   - Wait for confirmation message
   - Student now has access to the selected classroom

**Tips:**
- Students can be added to multiple classrooms
- Verify student email before assignment
- Students will see the classroom immediately on their dashboard

### 5.3 Removing Students from Classrooms

**When to Remove:**
- Student has completed the course
- Student is transferring to another class
- Temporary suspension from classroom

**Process:**

1. **Access Removal Page:**
   - Open side menu
   - Click "Remove Student from Classroom"

2. **Select Student and Classroom:**
   - Choose the student to remove
   - Select the classroom they should be removed from

3. **Confirm Removal:**
   - Click "Remove Student from Classroom" button
   - Confirm the action when prompted
   - Student loses access to that classroom immediately

**Important:**
- This action removes classroom access only, not the student account
- Student data and progress are preserved
- Can be reversed by re-adding the student

### 5.4 Modifying Student Details

**Editable Information:**
- Full name
- Telephone number
- Responsible party information
- Age
- Email address (with caution)

**Process:**

1. **Navigate to Modification Page:**
   - Open side menu
   - Select "Modify Student Details"

2. **Select Student:**
   - Choose student from dropdown list
   - Current information will populate the form

3. **Edit Details:**
   - Update the required fields
   - Ensure all information is accurate

4. **Save Changes:**
   - Click "Update" or "Save" button
   - Wait for confirmation message

**Cautions:**
- Changing email address affects student login
- Notify students of any credential changes
- Double-check all information before saving

### 5.5 Student Management Best Practices

**Organization:**
- Maintain an external record of student credentials
- Group students logically into classrooms
- Regularly update student contact information

**Communication:**
- Inform students of their login credentials securely
- Notify students when adding them to new classrooms
- Communicate any changes to student accounts promptly

**Security:**
- Never share student passwords publicly
- Use secure channels for credential distribution
- Regularly remind students to keep passwords private

## 6. Timetable and Session Management

### 6.1 Accessing Your Timetable

**Navigation:**
1. Open the side menu
2. Click "My Timetable"
3. View your weekly schedule

**Timetable Display:**
- Weekly calendar view
- Days: Monday through Sunday
- Sessions shown with time slots
- Classroom assignments visible

### 6.2 Adding a Session to Timetable

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Access Session Creation:**
   - From My Timetable, click "Add Session" or similar button
   - Navigate to "Add Session For Timetable"

2. **Select Day:**
   - Choose day of the week from dropdown
   - Options: Monday through Sunday

3. **Select Classroom:**
   - Choose the classroom for this session
   - Only your classrooms will appear in the list

4. **Set Time:**
   - **Start Time:** Select session start time
   - **End Time:** Select session end time
   - Ensure times don't overlap with existing sessions

5. **Add Session Details (if applicable):**
   - Session title or topic
   - Additional notes or description

6. **Save Session:**
   - Click "Add Session" or "Save" button
   - Confirm the session details
   - Session appears in your timetable immediately

**Time Management Tips:**
- Schedule sessions with 5-10 minute breaks between them
- Consider student time zones if applicable
- Plan buffer time for technical issues
- Keep consistent weekly schedules when possible

### 6.3 Viewing Session Details

**In Timetable View:**
- Click on any session to view details
- See classroom name, time, and duration
- View list of enrolled students

### 6.4 Deleting a Session

**When to Delete:**
- Session is cancelled
- Permanent schedule change
- Classroom is discontinued

**Process:**

1. **Navigate to Deletion Page:**
   - From side menu or timetable
   - Select "Delete Session from Timetable"

2. **Select Session:**
   - Choose the day
   - Select the session to delete
   - Confirm classroom and time

3. **Confirm Deletion:**
   - Click "Delete Session" button
   - Confirm when prompted
   - Session is removed immediately

**Important:**
- Deleted sessions cannot be recovered
- Consider rescheduling instead of deleting
- Notify students of cancelled sessions

### 6.5 Timetable Best Practices

**Planning:**
- Create timetable at the beginning of each term/month
- Share schedule with students in advance
- Maintain consistent weekly patterns

**Flexibility:**
- Build in break times
- Have backup session slots available
- Account for holidays and special events

**Communication:**
- Inform students of schedule changes promptly
- Post timetable in classroom comments if needed
- Send reminders before sessions

## 7. Notes and Homework Management

### 7.1 Understanding Notes vs. Homework

**Notes (Teaching Materials):**
- Uploaded by tutors/admins
- Visible to all students in the classroom
- Tagged with green "NOTE" badge
- Examples: Lesson plans, reference materials, worksheets

**Homework (Student Submissions):**
- Uploaded by students
- Visible only to uploading student and tutor
- Tagged with yellow "HOMEWORK" badge
- Privacy-protected between students

### 7.2 Uploading Notes (Teaching Materials)

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Navigate to Upload Page:**
   - From dashboard, click on a classroom card
   - Select "Upload Notes" button

2. **Select Classroom:**
   - Choose the classroom for this material
   - Verify correct classroom is selected

3. **Choose File:**
   - Click "Choose File" or "Browse" button
   - Select file from your computer
   - **Supported formats:** PDF, DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX, images

4. **Upload File:**
   - Click "Upload" or "Submit" button
   - Wait for upload progress to complete
   - Confirmation message appears when successful

**Upload Tips:**
- Keep file names descriptive
- Check file size limits (typically 10-25 MB)
- Preview files before uploading

### 7.3 Viewing Uploaded Notes

**Admin View Features:**
- See all notes with green "NOTE" badges
- See all student homework with yellow "HOMEWORK" badges
- View uploader email for each item
- Download or delete any content

### 7.4 Managing Homework Submissions

1. Navigate to "View Notes"
2. Look for yellow "HOMEWORK" badges
3. Check uploader email to identify student
4. Click to view/download and provide feedback
